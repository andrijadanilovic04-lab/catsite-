Why do we love cats?

Cats are independent, entertaining and often very creative in the way they get our attention. Whether you are looking for companionship or just want to watch funny videos — cats are a constant source of joy.

Quick facts

  • Cats can sleep up to 16 hours a day.
  • Their hearing and vision are extremely sharp — especially at dusk.
  • Cats use their whiskers to measure spaces and objects.

About cats

Cats (Felis catus) are small carnivorous mammals that have been living with humans for thousands of years. Adopting a cat brings joy but also responsibility — proper care, regular veterinary check-ups and enough stimulation are essential.

Health

Pay attention to nutrition, a clean litter box and regular vaccinations. If you notice a change in appetite or behavior — a visit to the vet is recommended.

Quick tips

  • Use high-quality, balanced food appropriate for age.
  • Play with your cat at least 10–20 minutes a day to encourage mental and physical activity.
  • Provide a scratching post to prevent furniture damage.
  • Clean the litter box regularly — cats appreciate cleanliness.
  • Monitor the condition of teeth and gums — oral health is important.
